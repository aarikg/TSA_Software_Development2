Technical Architecture

Frontend

  • HTML5, CSS3, JavaScript: The foundation of our responsive and interactive user interface.
  • Tailwind CSS: A utility-first framework for rapid, professional UI development.
  • Chart.js: For dynamic data visualization of soil and financial data.
  • Local Storage: Persists user's farm data directly in the browser for speed and privacy.

AI & Services

  • Google Gemini 1.5 Flash: A single, powerful AI model used for multiple, distinct tasks:
    • Multimodal Vision: Analyzing plant health from images.
    • Data Synthesis: Generating irrigation plans from crop, soil, and weather data.
    • Strategic Planning: Creating multi-year crop rotation schedules.
    • Live Data Retrieval: Estimating current market prices for commodities.
  • WeatherAPI.com: Delivers live, geo-located current weather and 3-day forecast data.
